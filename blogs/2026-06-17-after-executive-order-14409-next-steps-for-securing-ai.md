Following Executive Order 14409 signed June 2, 2026, CrowdStrike's Robert Sheldon outlines recommendations for AI security including deploying AI detection and response tools, strengthening identity protection, establishing a CISA-administered Sensitive Remediations Program for critical infrastructure, and proactive open source vulnerability scanning. He notes AI-enabled adversaries increased attacks by 89% year-over-year in 2025.
