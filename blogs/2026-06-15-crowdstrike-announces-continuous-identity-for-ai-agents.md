CrowdStrike introduces Continuous Identity for AI agents, extends privileged access into AWS cloud infrastructure, and unifies identity intelligence across human, non-human, and AI identities. The innovations address the limitations of authenticate-once models in environments where autonomous AI agents access systems, invoke APIs, and make decisions at machine speed.
